## Recommended Progression Paths

### For Casual Players (15-30 min sessions)
Focus on short playtime quests with occasional mid quests:
1. Complete Welcome Quest
2. Do daily short quests (1-3 per session)
3. Work on one mid quest across multiple sessions
4. Gradually accumulate resources for long quests

**Recommended Questlines:**
- Adventure (biome visits are quick)
- Catching (catch 1-3 PokÃ©mon per session)
- Utility (daily scanning, throwing)

### For Regular Players (1-2 hour sessions)
Balance of short and mid quests with occasional long quest progress:
1. Complete Welcome Quest
2. Knock out 3-5 short quests per session
3. Complete 1-2 mid quests per session
4. Make progress on 1-2 long quests

**Recommended Questlines:**
- All questlines in parallel
- Focus on type-specific quests
- Work toward collection milestones

### For Dedicated Players (3+ hours or regular long sessions)
Focus on completing entire chains and long-term goals:
1. Complete all short quests quickly
2. Chain mid quests together
3. Actively pursue long quest goals
4. Aim for master/legendary quests

**Recommended Questlines:**
- Complete entire questline chains
- Focus on capstone quests
- Collect all achievements
