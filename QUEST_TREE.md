## Quest Count by Difficulty

### Beginner (No dependencies beyond Welcome)
- Adventure: 6 quests (biome exploration)
- Catching: 14 quests (first catch, type catches, shiny)
- Battling: 10 quests (first battle, PvP, NPC, types)
- Progression: 7 quests (basic items, PC, herbs, apricorns, training)
- Utility: 9 quests (all utility tasks)
**Total: 46 beginner quests**

### Intermediate (1-2 dependencies)
- Adventure: 4 quests (advanced biomes)
- Catching: 3 quests (collection milestones)
- Battling: 4 quests (training progression, level challenges)
- Progression: 8 quests (better items, rainbow collection)
- Utility: 5 quests (advanced scanning, trading, research)
**Total: 24 intermediate quests**

### Advanced (Multiple dependencies/Capstones)
- Adventure: 2 quests (Biome Master, dimensions)
- Catching: 1 quest (Master Collector)
- Battling: 2 quests (Battle Master, Elite Challenger)
- Progression: 2 quests (Maximum Power, Crafting Master)
- Utility: 1 quest (Expert Researcher)
**Total: 8 advanced quests**

### Special/Rare
- Shiny Hunter (can be done anytime but very rare)
**Total: 1 special quest**

## Reward Tiers

### Common Rewards (5-10 items)
- Basic Poké Balls
- Potions
- Common materials

### Uncommon Rewards (10-20 items)
- Great/Ultra Balls
- Emeralds
- Better healing items

### Rare Rewards (Special items)
- Master Balls (awarded in 6 quests)
- Diamond Blocks (awarded in 3 quests)
- Large quantities (20+) of items

### Epic Rewards (Capstone quests)
- Multiple rare items combined
- Master Balls + Diamond Blocks + other items
- Exclusive to final/capstone quests
